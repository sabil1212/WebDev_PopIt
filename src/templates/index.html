What is Pop it?

Pop it literally means "pop this". It's a children's game that can be compared to popping bubble wrap packaging. But unlike regular bubble wrap, pop-its can be pressed with characteristic sensations and sounds "endlessly": dozens of silicone bubbles simply push out to the other side.
